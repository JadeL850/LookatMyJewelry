Harper has a very minimalist sense in terms of jewelry – the total opposite of her sense in shoes and scarves, any other accessory, basically.

“What’s the one word you would use to describe your collection in jewelry? “ I ask. There’s not even a moment of pause. Harper is a girl who knows what she wants. “I’d say love, friendship, and loyalty. Loyalty is everything [to me].”

01. Mom's Grad Ring

“This is my mom’s grad ring, from when she was in college in
the 90s”, Harper explained.

“I actually can’t remember if I ‘stole it’ from her or not,” she
chuckles, “but basically, I think that the point of me owning this
ring is that this is something quite personal to my mom,” she explains.

After close observation, I point out, “Look, you can even see her name!”
Her mom’s custom ring has engraved her name, and the two college
sports she used to play during her university years in Germany.

02. Birthstone Bracelet

“This is the first jewelry my dad
bought me”


“This is a David Yurman bracelet my dad got for me – this is the first
piece of jewelry my dad ever got for me, which is why it’s so special!”
Harper explains.

Knowing Harper’s clear love for David Yurman, its obvious why that’s
her signature bracelet. It’s “the” go-to.

She continues, “And this is my birth stone, which is blue topaz!”

It seems that carrying both jewelry from her parents portrays a theme
of security and good luck for Harper.

03. Grandma’s Heart Ring

“My grandma got this ring for me from a fair,” Harper explains.

There really is no standard in Harper’s jewelry when it comes to someone
she loves – in particular, her family.

Sometimes it also doesn’t matter what the jewelry is made of – it is more
like, what the jewelry represents, and what it holds.

04. Loyalty Ring

"This is the most important to me"

Towards the end of her succinct collection, I ask Harper, “So, if
you had to choose an ultimate theme across your favorite [jewelry]
pieces, how would you describe it?”

Without even a pause, Harper states, “I would probably say the love
I feel or have in my life.”

“All in all, I feel that the most important feature I look for in anything
– love, friends, and family – is loyalty.”
